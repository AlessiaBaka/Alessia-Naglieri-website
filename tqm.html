TQM brochure design

Client project of a brochure for TQM's master in tourism

An informative brochure

I made this brochure in collaboration with the copyrighters and marketing advisors of the agency Efficere.uno. We designed this brochure two years in a row for this company, for both of their branches in Rome and Milan, as well as restyling their logo.

Visual features

This brochure shows the potential of the master with a long description of their program, each divided visually by its own pattern and color.

Last but not least, I've designed a poster insert for each brochure containing many reviews from past students of this master. This poster had two versions, one designed to be folded (image below) and another to be hang on the school's walls .

This is the insert for the brochure, which has the same quote style of the brochure. In addition, all contacts needed to have information or join this course are included, resulting that the advertising works even if it's taken without the main brochure.
