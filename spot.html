Tv ad for Barilla

Stop-motion video

This video has been designed in collaboration with university peers. It has been submitted to a contest of the well-known italian pasta brand Barilla.

 

Layout and design choices

In order to emphasize the characteristics of Barilla's brand, we have used the combination of deep blue and bright yellow, which is part of their colour identity. The entire video is a metaphor for the creation of a recipe.
