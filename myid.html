Hi there! My name is Alessia, and I’m a Graphic Designer currently living in London

Since I was a little girl I’ve seen drawing as a demonstration of one’s ideas and thoughts, as well as traditional painting and photography.

Afterwards my High School diploma in decorative painting, I have attended Art and Design University in Rome. There I learned how to carry on many types of projects, digital, product and graphic design as well as honing traditional old printing and typographic methods.

I also have strong stakeholder management and communication skills having worked for many types of clients across different industries both in Italy and UK.

I'm passionate about languages and different cultures; I've had a six months university placement in Spain and lived for the last two years between Italy and the UK where I have now definitively relocated.

My target in each of my projects is achieving to combine beauty and functionality.
