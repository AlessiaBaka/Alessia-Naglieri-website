Hi there! My name is Alessia, and I’m a Graphic Designer currently living in London

Since I was a little girl I’ve seen the visual arts, traditional painting and photography alike, as the best reflection of one’s ideas and thoughts.

After obtaining my High School diploma in decorative painting, I have attended the "Accademia di Belle Arti di Roma", where I graduated with a first class honors in Editorial Design. There I've learnt how to carry on many types of projects: digital, product and graphic design as well as honing traditional old printing and typographic methods.

I also have strong stakeholder management and communication skills having worked for many types of clients across different industries both in Italy and UK.

I'm passionate about languages and different cultures; I've been in Spain for a six months university placement and, for the last two years, I've lived between Italy and the UK, where I have now definitively relocated.

My objective is to always strive to achieve the perfect combination of beauty and functionality.
