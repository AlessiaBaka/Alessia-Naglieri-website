Magazine design and UX

Magazine project for mixed medias

The design project for this magazine is modern and fresh. This a fencing magazine with a cover story talking about historical fencing. The two covers above show the different target of which they are intended. For the subscribers cover, since the cover story is about historical fencing, I’ve opted for an illustrated cover resembling a herald. Meanwhile the newsstand version has an engaging and powerful photography representing a black glove with a longsword. The grid for the internal pieces is composed of three columns and the illustrations are built in it creating a balance between text and visual parts.

Logo adaptation for device

This is a mockup of the icon for this magazine in the Apple store, both for tablet and smartphones.

Magazine’s device adaptation

Device adaptation has been a challenge to me. The grid has been maintained to three columns, but the text flow mostly covers them all for keeping legibility. The covers and service has been adapted for each device in portrait and landscape as you can see in some examples below. And the splash page is the loading page for the magazine.
