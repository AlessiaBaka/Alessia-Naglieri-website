Distopia

Series of photographic artist's books

Insight

Distopia (Italian for dystopia) is a visual experience through a dystopic world destined to shatter. These books have three different hand-made bindings, two Japanese and a crown, as shown left.

Visual features

To create the books' visuals I’ve used the Japanese technique suminagashi (or “floating ink”) on many different types of paper. The one shown below is rice paper, combined with prints of photographies, creating beautiful overlapping effects.

DA RIFORMULARE

These books are complementary: they include one with only suminagashi, one only photographic, and the last with a combination of the two and a tale.
